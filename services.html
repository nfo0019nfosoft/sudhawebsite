Services

General And
Laparoscopic Surgery

Our surgical team performs a wide range of procedures using both traditional and minimally invasive techniques. Laparoscopic surgery offers faster recovery, less pain, and minimal scarring for eligible patients We focus on precision, safety, and patient comfort from pre-op to post-op care. Whether it’s hernia repair or gallbladder removal, you’re in expert hands. Every surgery is guided by the highest standards of clinical excellence.

Obstetrics And
Gynaecology Infertility

We offer complete care for women—from routine checkups to advanced fertility treatments. Our obstetricians provide personalized support throughout pregnancy, delivery, and postnatal care. With advanced infertility services, we help couples achieve their dream of parenthood. Our gynecology team also treats conditions like PCOS, fibroids, and menstrual disorders. Comfort, privacy, and compassion are at the heart of our women’s health services.

Orthopaedics And
Trauma

Our orthopaedic unit specializes in bone, joint, and spine care, from fractures to joint replacements. We handle trauma cases with precision, ensuring timely interventions and rehabilitation support. Whether it’s sports injuries, arthritis, or chronic pain, we provide long-term solutions. Our team uses modern surgical techniques and imaging for accurate diagnosis and care. Regain mobility and strength with our expert orthopaedic care.

Urology

We treat conditions related to the urinary tract and male reproductive system with advanced urological care. From kidney stones to urinary infections and prostate issues, we offer comprehensive treatment plans. Minimally invasive procedures are available for quicker recovery and less discomfort. Our specialists prioritize privacy, patient comfort, and accurate diagnostics. We’re committed to improving quality of life through expert urological care.

Nephrology

Our nephrology department manages kidney-related diseases with expert medical and dialysis care. We diagnose and treat conditions like chronic kidney disease, hypertension, and electrolyte imbalances. Dialysis services are provided in a clean, comfortable setting with skilled supervision. We focus on early intervention and long-term management to protect kidney function. Patient education and personalized care are integral to our approach.

Paediatric Surgery

Our pediatric surgeons offer safe, specialized surgical care for infants, children, and adolescents. From birth defects to emergency surgeries, we ensure gentle, precise interventions. We work closely with pediatricians and anesthetists for holistic child care. Advanced equipment and child-friendly environments make recovery easier and faster. Your child’s comfort, safety, and well-being are our top priorities.

Pulmonology

We offer expert care for lung and respiratory disorders like asthma, COPD, and pneumonia. Pulmonary function testing, bronchoscopy, and advanced imaging support accurate diagnosis. Our pulmonologists develop personalized treatment plans for each patient’s needs. We also provide critical care for respiratory failure and long-term lung diseases. Breathe easy with care that’s focused on improving your lung health.

Paediatrics

Our pediatric department provides comprehensive care from birth to adolescence. Services include routine checkups, immunizations, nutrition advice, and treatment of common illnesses. We prioritize early detection and prevention to support your child’s growth and development. Our child-friendly environment makes visits easier for both kids and parents. Every child is treated with compassion, patience, and personalized attention.

Anaesthesia &
Critical Care

Our anaesthesia team ensures patient safety and comfort during surgeries and medical procedures. We use modern techniques and continuous monitoring to manage pain and sedation effectively. The critical care unit provides life support and advanced monitoring for seriously ill patients. Staffed by ICU specialists and equipped with cutting-edge systems, we offer intensive, round-the-clock care. Every second matters—and our team is always prepared.

ENT

We provide expert diagnosis and treatment for conditions of the ear, nose, and throat. From sinus infections and hearing loss to tonsil removal and voice disorders, we handle it all. Our ENT specialists use advanced tools for accurate assessments and minimally invasive procedures. Both children and adults receive care tailored to their specific condition. Hear, breathe, and speak better with our dedicated ENT services.

General Medicine

Our general medicine team is your first point of contact for a wide range of health concerns. We diagnose and manage chronic conditions like diabetes, hypertension, infections, and more. With a focus on prevention and holistic care, we guide patients through every stage of treatment. Lab tests, imaging, and consultations are streamlined for quick results. Your long-term health is our long-term goal.

24/7 Emergency

Our emergency department operates 24/7, providing immediate care for critical and life-threatening conditions. Equipped with advanced medical tools and rapid response teams, we handle trauma, cardiac events, and more. Trained doctors and nurses are available at all times to ensure fast and accurate treatment. Your safety is our top priority.
